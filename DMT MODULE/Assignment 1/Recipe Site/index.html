Chocolate cupcakes

Ingredients:

  • 4 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 1/4 cup vegetable oil
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1 cup all purpose flour
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/4 cup plus 2 tablespoons unsweeten natuaral cocoa powder
  • 3/4 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/8 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 1 large egg
  • 1/4 cup buttermilk
  • teaspoon pure vanilla extract

    Direction:

  1. Gather all the ingredients
  2. Preheat the oven to 350Â°F. Line a 12-cup muffin tin with paper or foil liners
  3. In a medium saucepan, melt the butter with the vegetable oil and water over low heat
  4. In a large bowl, sift the flour with the sugar, cocoa powder, baking soda and salt
  5. Add melted butter and beat at low speed until smooth
  6. Add the egg and beat until incorporated, then add the buttermilk and vanilla and beat until smooth
  7. Pour the batter into the lined muffin tins, filling them about three-fourths full.
  8. Bake the cupcakes in the center of the oven for about 25 minutes
  9. Frost and top the cupcakes as desired
